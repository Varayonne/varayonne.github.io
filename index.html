History

The Corladien Republic is a country in the Caidfyr region. They have a large cultural, political, military, and economic influence on the world. The country achieved the Majesty of Civilizations (a term referring to a state's ability to project power) in the 1860s with advanced military developments and political reforms.

Early History

Much of the early history boils down to the formation of several Corisch tribes. The most dominant of these being the Royenne, Lananncor, and Edrieu. It took several decades for laws to be codified and a more formal societal structure to form, but once it did, the Lananncors became the leading tribe and eventually the leading dynasty for the crown. Ysoirre I was the first to crown himself as King of the Corisch.

FAQ

  1. How do I make a submission?
    2. In order to submit, your work must demonstrate adequate knowledge and respect for the world. Now, that doesn't mean we're going to ask you to take a test or anything, but if you add fully automatic laser guns, it's going to get declined. You also must meet certain standards of quality when it comes to writing, which mainly comes down to the essentials, such as spelling, grammar, and whatnot. Everyone makes mstakes, so if it's small it will likely get edited. But if it's typo after typo after typo, you'll be asked to rewrite.

  2. Do you own what I submit?
    3. Nope, nope, and nope. What you write is 100% yours, forever. I can't take that away from you. Even if there are minor edits from others involved, you are still credited as the creator.

  3. I'm writing and I wanted to know, is X,Y,Z technology/magic/item allowed?

    4. This gets back into the "adequate knowledge" for making submissions. It's impossible to cover every little detail, but keep in mind that the tech is early 1900s. However, there are fantastical gadgets. If you are curious, it's best to ask on a case-by-case basis. But remember that there aren't many fully automatic machineguns, there are no assault rifles, and cars are still a luxury. In this world, a lot of people still travel between landmasses by airships (which are also expensive, but tickets are cheap). This is because the world consists of suspended, floating islands. Lots of them.

  4. Can I add X,Y,Z city/town/location/faction/gang?
    5. The answer to this is most likely "yes." Again, ask if you're worried. As long as it isn't a faction that changes the power dynamics of the world, you're likely in the clear. If you're writing about conflict (wars, battles, fights) then be cautious. Don't introduce a major conflict that changes the geopolitical climate or anything.

  5. What themes/genres are allowed?
    6. This is a serious world. While you can make the occasional joke or jab, this isn't a comedic series. This isn't also one that lends itself to romance, but it may have romantic moments. This is mainly an adventure series with some action elements. That said, we are not looking for gorefests or extremely sexual content. If there are three pages describing the details of a bullet wound, it's not going to get accepted. Themes can vary from war, science, psychology, the political and relatable socioeconomic strains a nation faces due to the worsening divide between the rich and poor. Almost anything, really.

  6. How long should my story be?
    7. We are looking for material in the short story range to around the novella range. Now, those words mean nothing, because websites can tell you varying amounts. So, for word counts, a minimum of 4k words and a maximum of 25k words.
